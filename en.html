Corrugated cardboard

Cardboard boxes

Four-valve corrugated boxes, according to the catalog FEFCO 0201 is the most common type of packaging for transportation of products.

Provides good product protection during assembly, shipment and storage.

Cardboard cases

Trays with an open or semi-open top, boxes with a separate lid, show boxes, wrappers.

Any sizes and configurations according to FEFCO catalog or customer's drawing are possible.

Large-size packaging

Large corrugated boxes and octabins allow you to effectively protect products from damage during transportation.

This kind of packaging is light weight, made of five-layer corrugated cardboard and, if necessary, further strengthened on the sides.

Accessories

For the safe storage and transportation of products in boxes use additional packaging.

Pads, liners and grids help to provide extra strength to the boxes, as well as to separate the goods from within.

Paper bags

Valve paper bag

Valve paper bags are glued bags up and down having one hole with a valve for filling products. And it is a great option for storing any bulk materials used in the food industry, construction or agriculture. Also, kraft bags with an external valve are suitable for storage of chemical materials, soda, gypsum, cement, dry building mixtures, starch, etc.

Glued-bottom paper sack

bags with an open mouth for packing products. Kraft paper is used for manufacturing paper bags in our production.

Glued-bottom paper sack are used to store seeds, chemical raw materials, organic fertilizers, charcoal, feed, dry milk, casein, frozen berries, crackers, pasta, etc.

Paper sacks for charcoal

In order to maintain the qualitative characteristics of the coal and extend the life of its use, you should provide proper packaging. Our company manufactures kraft paper bags with full-color printing.
This packaging is practical and environmentally friendly, it allows to store the properties of coal and its quality.

Single face board

Is stored in rolls, is a universal packing material. Allows you to ensure the safety of different product groups during transportation. Corrugated cardboard in rolls has an affordable price, also takes up little space during transportation and storage, does not require additional packaging when transporting due to its durability. The roll width is 1250mm and 1050 mm. Our company offers both options for choice.

Single wall board

Single wall cardboard is intended for making corrugated boxes and for packing various kinds of products. Used as corrugated board. Depending on strength and rigidity, it is classified on the stamps. The width of the sheet of corrugated cardboard ̶ is up to 2400 mm. The superficial layer may be brown or white.

Corrugated cardboard consists of two flat and one corrugated layer with corrugated B, C or E flute. Corrugated cardboard of E flute is also called micro-corrugated cardboard.

Double wall board

Is intended for manufacturing of large-sized, high-strength and rigid containers, containers. It consists of three flat and two corrugated layers with a combination of corrugated E / V, E / C and B / C flutes. The width of the sheet of corrugated cardboard ̶ is up to 2400 mm. The superficial layer may be brown or white.

Flexoprinting

Zakhid Kraft Resource uses flexo equipment from the Italian company Bielloni Castello with a wide range of capabilities, which allows the printing of six-color water-based paints on a roll up to 1050 mm wide.

Flexographic printing is one of the most popular and popular ways of drawing images, logos and inscriptions on paper. With the help of Flexo printing service, Kraft paper acquires a more presentable and attractive appearance.
